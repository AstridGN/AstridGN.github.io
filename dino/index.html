Dino

2020



Dino is a porcelain pitcher with fully function but with an unsteady grip due to a large radius that excludes small hands. This could be provocative since you need a two-handed grip. The grip you embrace the pitcher with is also advanced and non-ergonomic due to the “backbone” constructed with thorns. The pitcher is more aesthetically appealed than practically useful, that’s why Dino also fits perfect as a vase with flowers.
