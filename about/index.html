I am Astrid, I will soon start my internship at HAY accessories department in Copenhagen. My practice currently investigates historical and contemporary materials and methods. My purpose often involves pushing boundaries, striving towards a field where I might not feel safe – a territory where I challenge former, prevalent conventions and replace, or further question them by experimental and sometimes provocative methods and results.
