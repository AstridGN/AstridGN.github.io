Bubbles of Glass

2021



Bubblor av Glas (Bubbles of Glas) is a series of glass vases made in Bergdalahyttan, Småland, Sweden. Each vase was designed with emphasis put on the vase’s own self expression. The production process incorporates spontaneity, resulting in vases with various, arbitrary shapes.
