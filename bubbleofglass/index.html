Bubbles of Glass

2021



One of the goals of mass production is to produce glass products that maintain a stable manufacturing process and produces identical products. This could also be done in a glass factory when you blow the glass into a given shape. “Bubblor av Glas” (Bubbles of Glass) is a series of glass vases made in Bergdalahyttan, Småland, Sweden. Each vase has been allowed to have its own expression. All bubbles are made in different sizes, heights and colors.
